Event Details

This showcase is designed for students who are learning how to move from basic web pages to more intentional interfaces. We will look at real student work, talk about what makes an interface feel usable, and discuss how CSS choices affect the final experience. The event is casual and beginner-friendly, but it should still feel organized, polished, and easy to understand.

  • Date: Thursday, May 21
  • Time: 6:00 PM – 8:00 PM
  • Location: Design Lab, Room B204
  • Cost: Free for students

What We’ll Do

Portfolio Walkthroughs

Students will share short walkthroughs of portfolio pages they built in previous courses.

Live CSS Fixes

We will take one messy interface and improve the design using only CSS.

Peer Feedback

Small groups will discuss what feels clear, confusing, polished, or unfinished.
